# End-to-End UI Flow: Hybrid EdTech Platform

This document outlines the complete user journeys for each role within the application.

## 1. Authentication & Onboarding (Common Flow)
All new users follow this sequence to establish their account:

1.  **Registration**: Sign up at `/register`.
2.  **Verification**: Confirm identity via OTP at `/verify-otp`.
3.  **Setup**: Set account password at `/set-password`.
4.  **Login**: Access the system at `/login`.
5.  **Direction**: Based on role, users are routed to their respective home/dashboard.

---

## 2. Student Journey: Finding and Booking a Tutor
The core value proposition for students is discovering and booking educational sessions.

1.  **Discovery**:
    *   **Find Tutors (`/tutors`)**: Browse the marketplace.
    *   **Filter/Search**: Narrow down by Subject, Mode (Online/Offline/Hybrid), and City.
2.  **Selection**:
    *   **Tutor Profile (`/tutors/[id]`)**: View detailed bio, qualifications, subjects, and ratings.
3.  **Booking**:
    *   **Select Slot**: Pick from available calendar slots provided by the tutor.
    *   **Confirmation**: Complete the booking process.
4.  **Management**:
    *   **My Bookings (`/bookings`)**: View upcoming sessions, track status, and manage history.

---

## 3. Tutor Journey: Profile and Availability Management
Tutors focus on setting up their teaching profile and managing student requests.

1.  **Onboarding**:
    *   **Tutor Dashboard (`/tutor/dashboard`)**: Overview of stats and upcoming tasks.
    *   **Complete Profile (`/tutor/profile`)**: Fill in bio, education, and subjects.
    *   **Submit Verification (`/tutor/verification`)**: Upload ID and certifications for admin review.
2.  **Scheduling**:
    *   **Availability (`/tutor/availability`)**: Configure weekly slots or specific dates.
3.  **Operations**:
    *   **Manage Bookings (`/tutor/bookings`)**: Accept/Decline requests and view session details.

---

## 4. Admin Journey: Platform Oversight
Administrators ensure the quality and safety of the marketplace.

1.  **Overview**:
    *   **Admin Dashboard (`/admin/dashboard`)**: System-wide analytics (Total users, tutors, active bookings).
2.  **Moderation**:
    *   **User Management (`/admin/users`)**: View and manage all student and tutor accounts.
    *   **Verification Review (`/admin/verification`)**: Review documents submitted by tutors to grant "Verified" status.
3.  **Analytics**:
    *   **Reports (`/admin/reports`)**: Deep dive into platform performance and financial summaries.
